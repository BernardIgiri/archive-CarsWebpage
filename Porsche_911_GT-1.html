Beyond the specs:

The wildly famous Porsche 911 GT-1, this car has won more Le Mans races than any other competing racecar. I am not sure, but I think its secrete is the ability to survive the 24 hours of racing, its 4wd, and its excellent drivers. This car is a perfect example of the engineering skill of Porsche, this car has the smallest engine in the entire GT1 class (only 6 cylinders!), but some how it still wins very often with its rev happy engine. (The competition has much low-end torque.) Unfortunately, this car has been taken out of the Le Mans race for this year by Porsche, but that is only so the can work on its replacement. The replacement car will look more like the current 911 and will have a 600hp 5-liter twin turbo V8.
