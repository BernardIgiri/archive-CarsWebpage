Beyond the specs:

The Chevrolet C5 Corvette, the classic American sports car in its latest iteration, a great improvement over its predecessor. A Corvette in which the term refined can be freely while allowing it to keep its musclecar image. This Corvette is faster, more comfortable, more refined, and now actually handles very well. This car belongs here because it offers excellent performance at a bargain price ($40,000!). Moreover, as Callaway has shown, its has even greater performance potential and since it is so famous there are many after market modifications available for it. In addition, it now comes with a stability control system to prevent spinouts and understeer. Therefore, if you do not want to have to spend over $50,000 (even the convertible cost less than that) and want the performance of a true sports car then this car will fit you perfectly.
