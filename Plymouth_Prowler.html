Beyond the specs:

The hotrod you can by at a dealership, the Plymouth Prowler with its very controversial appearance, is it ugly or is it beautiful? That all depends on you, but one thing is sure, it will attract attention wherever you go. This car has massive tires, 17in front tires and 21in rear tires, but with only 253hp these tries are obviously only there for their appearance. This car was intended to be a lightweight but it turned out a little heavier than planned, still its weight is pretty light allowing the V6 engine to propel it to 60mph in under 6 seconds. The previous prowler had a much weaker version of the same engine and it took a full 7 seconds to sixty, but now with its new engine it is now a real sports car. This vehicle has good handling and is easy to control with its grippy tires and double wishbone suspension and rear multi-link suspension. This car with its huge tires can fit much lager brakes than the 13in rear and 11.1in front disks it comes with. In addition, I am sure many people will likely add a supercharger or a turbo charger to this car, after all, it is a hotrod. Customizing this car would probably be fun especially since its lightweight means its performance can greatly improve from small horsepower increases. This car is best for those who like the hot rod look but do not have the time to build their own or just want one that's safe and reliable enough for everyday street use.
