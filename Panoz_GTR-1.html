Beyond the specs:

The odd but beautiful Panoz GTR-1, this Ford powered car is another creation of the young car company Panoz. While I do not have much info on this car I do know for a fact that this car regularly in the GT1 class of Le Mans racing against the Mercedes CLK-GTR and the Mclaren F1 GTR. This car has competed with the Porsche 911 GT-1 too, but I do not know how successful it has been. As you can see from the info above it has an average level of power and has an average top speed.
