Beyond the specs:

The Acura NSX, though its design is beginning to show its age it is still a high tech very refined exotic supercar. This car may be fast, but not once you consider the $80,000+ price tag. But you must remember that with this car you not only get performance but you get mild luxury and a very comfortable driving position with an amazing 312° view (surpasses most cars). Another bonus is the quality that comes with the fact that each part of this car is made one by one by hand. In the production of the NSX, each worker gets as much time as necessary to make sure that the part is worthy of being in an NSX. Most importantly though, is its handling which makes it fairly easy to drive the car fast and while giving you adequate warning when you start to exceed the cars limits. This car comes in three versions, the regular NSX (has a 3.0L V6), the NXS-T (with a bigger engine), and the Zanardi Edition (small modifications and 149 fewer lbs.). The Zanardi Edition was built in celebration of the wins in CART racing Alex Zanardi (racecar driver) brought Honda. (Acura's parent company)
