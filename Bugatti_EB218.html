Beyond the specs:

Bugatti now back from this dead, raised up from the ashes by VW's eccentric owner Ferdinand Piëch and fortunately along with the name, he brought back the soul of the company too. As you can see from the stats of this car Mr. Piëch is quite eccentric, for who would put 18 cylinders in a sedan who's weight has been reduced by use of aluminum. (Just like on the Audi S8 another VW owned company.) The result of course is a sedan so amazingly fast that according to computer simulations no sports car short of the Porsche GT3 can out accelerate it. Its wonderful engine sounds like a turbine and idles at an amazing 3000rpm close to half its 8000-rpm redline! It is still a prototype however its competition has already been set, Rolls Royce is its competition. Yes, this amazingly fast sedan will have the luxury to compete against a car that is perhaps most luxuriest car in the world the Rolls Royce Silver Seraph. Which of course is a lot slower and cost upwards of $300,000, this Bugatti will also have a handling (as expected) and all wheel drive advantage over the Rolls Royce and it will have just as much interior space. However, you will have to pay for it, though the number probably will not exceed $300,000 by very much. This vehicle is obviously; "The best sedan money can buy". I am just waiting for the pricing and fuel economy numbers to be released to the public.
